Who am I?

As you’ve most likely guessed at this point, my name is Rebecca. I’m an 18yo accountant and photographer from Sweden.

I’ve for a long time been interested in accountancy and photography – with accounting I first started to do the family business’ taxes when I was 15 and been doing them ever since then.

In 2021 I thought I could do with some formal qualifications, so I started studying with ACCA on an RQFL 2 course which was great fun.

Currently I’m enrolled in a Level 7 course in accountancy which has been going well and it has been really interesting. Additionally, I have been a member of the Swedish Accountant Union since the summer of 2021, where I’ve gotten familiar with REX – the Swedish (and Nordic) accounting standards.

I first started photography when I was about 10, using one of my dad’s old SLR’s, fully manual, which naturally was a good learning experience. That camera is still in use today and is the one all my 35mm film photos have been taken with.

So, considering I had been doing photography and accounting for a while at that point, I decided in the autumn of 2021 to register a business as a sole proprietorship in order to be able to sell my services to anyone wanting to buy them.

If you’d be interested in having me prepare your accounts or perhaps take some photos for you be sure to send me a message, my contact information is below.
